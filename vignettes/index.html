Description: MetaboLights is a database for Metabolomics experiments and derived information. 
   The database is cross-species, cross-technique and covers metabolite structures and their 
   reference spectra as well as their biological roles, locations and concentrations, and 
   experimental data from metabolic experiments. MetaboLights is the recommended Metabolomics 
   repository for a number of leading journals.
